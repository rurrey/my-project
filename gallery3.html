Memeable Art

This gallery presents pieces of art that have high meme potential. Even though there has been critisism for using art as memes, it has been shown several times how a memable art piece have raisen in great popularity. This also attracts more visiter - especially amongst the youth - to visit the museums, which helps the work of preserving the art.
