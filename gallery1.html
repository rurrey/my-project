Flowers And Still Life Art

This gallery's digital selection has been carefully chosen amongst Nationalmusem's collection of still life art with flowers as a central element in the work. The pictures in are linked and will lead to WikiMedia's archive, where you can find more detailed information about the artwork. The pictures fall under public domain. The live versions can be enjoyed at Nationalmuseum in Stockholm.
